This project explores the data collected across the globe from a psychometric survey that totals to over 1 million participants!



The Big 5 Personality Traits are Agreeableness, Extraversion, Conscientiousness, Emotional Stability, and Intellect


High Agreeableness means trusting and helpful while low means suspicious and antagonistic.

High Extraversion means sociable and outward while low means reserved and thoughtful.

High Conscientiousness means careful and disciplined while low means impulsive and disorganized .

High Emotional Stability means calm and confident while low means anxious and neurotic.

High Intellect means imaginative and spontaneous while low means structured and practical.
